Writing fiction is a truly beautiful, amazing, satisfying thing. Imagine putting your own words on a page to tell a story. Which enters your readers' minds and allows them to share the thoughts emotions and lives of your characers. It is a very rewarding experience.

Here, we show you how can you write your first novel.
Many people say they have a great desire to write a novel but very few actually ever do it. But now you will do it!
1...You will learn how to form a basic idea, any idea, into a structure and discipline that will allow you to create, write, and complete a fully realized novel, ready to submit it for publication.
2...You'll learn to break down the creative process into components. And you'll discover a way of writing that will allow you to do what few have done.
3...You will learn by doing, and that is writing.

This is a step-by-step process that is essential to your success as a writer.
The only way to become a writer is to write, write, write. You need to unleash your creativity.
